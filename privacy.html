HR App Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines the information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Collection of Information:

  • Personal Information: When you download and use our HR app, we may collect personal information that you provide voluntarily, such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other details you choose to share. This information is used to create your account, personalize your experience, and communicate with you.
  • Device Information: We automatically collect certain information about your device when you use our app, including the device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, IP address, and mobile network information. This data helps us optimize the app for your specific device and troubleshoot any technical issues.
  • Usage Data: We may also collect information about how you interact with our app, such as the features you use, the content you view, and the actions you take. This data is used to analyze user behavior, improve the app's functionality, and enhance your overall experience.

2. Use of Information:

  • Personal Information: We use your personal information to provide you with access to our app's features and services, personalize your user experience, respond to your inquiries and support requests, send you notifications and updates, and comply with legal obligations.
  • Device Information: The device information we collect is used to ensure the proper functioning of the app on your device, troubleshoot technical issues, analyze app performance, and improve our services.
  • Usage Data: We analyze usage data to understand how users interact with our app, identify trends and patterns, improve app performance and functionality, and tailor content and features to better meet user needs.

3. Sharing of Information:

  • We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes without your consent.
  • We may share your personal information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in delivering our services, such as hosting providers, payment processors, analytics providers, and customer support services.
  • We may share aggregated and anonymized data with third parties for analytical or research purposes, but this data does not identify you personally.

4. Security Measures:

  • We take appropriate measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include encryption, access controls, secure data storage practices, and regular security audits.
  • While we strive to safeguard your information, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Changes to Privacy Policy:

  • We reserve the right to update our privacy policy as needed to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be communicated to users through the app or website.
  • By continuing to use our HR app after any changes to the privacy policy have been made, you agree to the updated terms.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy for the HR app or how we handle your data, please contact us at [info@realteethclinic.com]. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your information and ensuring a positive user experience.
